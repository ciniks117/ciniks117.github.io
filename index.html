Nikhil Kumar Singh

PhD Student

Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Kanpur

I am a PhD student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the IIT Kanpur under Indranil Saha. I am a recipient of the Prime Minister Research Fellowship since December-2020. I am working on synthesis of safe and robust controllers for safety-critical CPSs. I completed my MS (Research) from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, at IIT Kanpur, where I worked on development of an automated debugger for CPS models. I have worked at OptumSoft, Bangalore for 2 years, where I developed application software for distributed systems.
